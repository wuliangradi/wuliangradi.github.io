期望 均值 有了概率密度分布就很自然地求解期望值
期望和方差的公式:

$$
{\displaystyle \operatorname {E} [X]=\sum {i=1}^{\infty }x{i}\,p_{i}}
$$

$$
{\displaystyle \operatorname {E} [X]=\int _{-\infty }^{\infty }xf(x)\,dx.}
$$

f(x)是概率密度函数

$$
{\displaystyle \operatorname {E} [g(X)]=\int _{-\infty }^{\infty }g(x)f(x)\,dx.}
$$

$$
\operatorname {Var} (X)=\operatorname {E} \left[(X-\mu )^{2}\right]
$$

$$
\operatorname {Var} (X)=\sum {i=1}^{n}p{i}\cdot (x_{i}-\mu )^{2}
$$

$$
\operatorname {Var} (g(x))=\operatorname {E} \left[(f(x)-\operatorname {E}[f(x)])^{2}\right]
$$

$$
{\displaystyle {\begin{aligned}\operatorname {Var} (X)&==\operatorname {E} \left[X^{2}\right]-\operatorname {E} [X]^{2}\end{aligned}}}
$$

无论怎么变换,概率密度函数怎么复杂,随机变量是X
